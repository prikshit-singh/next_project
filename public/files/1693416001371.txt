It’s usually a way to communicate product quality.<em style="box-sizing: inherit; font-style: italic;">Our product is so good we didn’t have to advertise.</em></p><p style="box-sizing: inherit; margin: 2em 0px -0.46em; font-weight: 400; color: rgb(36, 36, 36); word-break: break-word; font-family: source-serif-pro, Georgia, Cambria, &quot;Times New Roman&quot;, Times, serif; font-style: normal; line-height: 32px; letter-spacing: -0.003em; font-size: 20px; font-variant-ligatures: normal; font-variant-caps: normal; orphans: 2; text-align: start; text-indent: 0px; text-transform: none; widows: 2; word-spacing: 0px; -webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px; white-space: normal; background-color: rgb(255, 255, 255); text-decoration-thickness: initial; text-decoration-style: initial; text-decoration-color: initial;">Ads are expensive. So not advertising is an attractive business strategy. But just because you don’t advertise, doesn’t mean you don’t market. Every company needs some sort of customer acquisition strategy. It just doesn’t need to be advertising.</p><p style="box-sizing: inherit; margin: 2em 0px -0.46em; font-weight: 400; color: rgb(36, 36, 36); word-break: break-word; font-family: source-serif-pro, Georgia, Cambria, &quot;Times New Roman&quot;, Times, serif; font-style: normal; line-height: 32px; letter-spacing: -0.003em; font-size: 20px; font-variant-ligatures: normal; font-variant-caps: normal; orphans: 2; text-align: start; text-indent: 0px; text-transform: none; widows: 2; word-spacing: 0px; -webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px; white-space: normal; background-color: rgb(255, 255, 255); text-decoration-thickness: initial; text-decoration-style: initial; text-decoration-color: initial;">In this case, the non-advertising brand is Van Leeuwen, an artisanal ice cream brand that started with a single cart in 2008 and now boasts 50 locations across the US, plus retail distribution in stores from WalMart to Whole Foods. Co-founder Ben Van Leeuwen started the brand as a way to offer ice cream made up of only simple, high quality ingredients.
